Travel & Trekking: Discover the Adventure!

Traveling and trekking open doors to new experiences, breathtaking landscapes, and unforgettable memories. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie or a nature lover, Tamil Nadu offers incredible travel destinations, from lush green hills to serene waterfalls.

Top Trekking Destinations Near Chennai (150km - 250km)

1. Nagalapuram (75 km)

Why Visit? A weekend trek with dense forests, waterfalls, and natural pools. The trek is moderately challenging and offers refreshing swimming spots.

Difficulty Level: Moderate

Best Time to Visit: October – March

2. Tada Falls / Ubbalamadugu Falls (92 km)

Why Visit? A hidden paradise with crystal-clear waters and rocky terrain, perfect for trekking and nature photography.

Difficulty Level: Easy to Moderate

Best Time to Visit: November – February

3. Yelagiri Hills (220 km)

Why Visit? A serene hill station known for the Swamimalai trek, panoramic views, paragliding, boating, and nature walks.

Difficulty Level: Easy

Best Time to Visit: October – February

4. Jawadhu Hills (200 km)

Why Visit? Less crowded and rich in tribal culture, with peaceful trekking trails and waterfalls.

Difficulty Level: Moderate

Best Time to Visit: August – January

5. Kolli Hills (250 km)

Why Visit? Known as the ‘Mountains of Death’ due to its 70 hairpin bends, Kolli Hills offers challenging treks, mesmerizing viewpoints, and the stunning Agaya Gangai Waterfalls.

Difficulty Level: Moderate to Difficult

Best Time to Visit: July – December

Trekking Tips & Essentials

Packing List:

  • ✅ Comfortable trekking shoes 🥾
  • ✅ Lightweight backpack 🎒
  • ✅ Water bottles (at least 2 liters) 💧
  • ✅ Energy bars & snacks 🍫
  • ✅ First aid kit 🚑
  • ✅ Trekking pole (for steep trails) 🏔️
  • ✅ Extra pair of clothes & rain cover ☔
  • ✅ Torchlight & Power bank 🔦

Safety Tips:

  • 🔹 Start early to avoid trekking in the dark
  • 🔹 Always trek in groups or with a guide
  • 🔹 Respect nature—avoid littering
  • 🔹 Stay hydrated and take regular breaks
  • 🔹 Inform someone about your trek plan before heading out
